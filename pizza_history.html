Pizza History

This page is here to give you a little more information on pizza. You can read about its history, famous pizza styles and a few of the notable pizza places in history. Its also a place where im going to be testing javascript, so please enjoy!

Pizza got started in the 1980's by a man named Joey Pizza. Finding himself in possesion of an obscene amount of bread dough, he was lost as to what to use it for. Talking with a friend, who had recently come across a large pile of tomatoes and garlic, and his wife, the local cheese maker who had seen a recent decline in sales, Joey came up with a sandwich that would change the world...
